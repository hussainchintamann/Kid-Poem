Teddy Bear, Teddy Bear

Teddy bear,teddy bear,
Turn around.
Teddy bear,teddy bear,
Touch the ground
Teddy bear,teddy bear,
Shine your shoes.
Teddy bear,teddy bear,
Skidoo.

Teddy bear,teddy bear,
Go upstairs.
Teddy bear,teddy bear,
Say your prayers.
Teddy bear,teddy bear,
Turn out the light.
Teddy bear,teddy bear,
Say good night.
